We provide a web-accessible database with information pertaining to the opioid crisis for government officials and healthcare providers.

What data is included?

  • Drug prescribers*: Name, Gender, Credentials, Location, Specialty.
  • Drugs: Name, Type (i.e. IsOpiate).
  • Quantities of each drugs that is prescribed by each Doctor.
  • Opioid overdose death rate for each state

We feed the above data into our machine learning algorithms to identify doctors who are over-prescribing opioids or other harmful drugs

*Drug prescribers: Examples of health providers include doctors, physician assistants, and nurse practitioners.
